Reduce costs and maximise revenue with managed parking in the cloud, no meters required.

The most convenient way to pay for parking. No tickets. No coins.

We've made it simple for customers to pay for parking using their smart phone. After syncing your licence plate number and credit card, you can check-in and pay for parking with the press of a button.

Check and validate parking spaces from the palm of your hand.

The CloudParx iPad application makes it easy for Parking Officers to reconcile payments with parking spaces. Our intuitive interface allows you to easily see who has paid or overstayed, and it works seamlessly with traditional ticketing machines.

Smart notifications give customers a better parking experience.

Once customers have checked in to their space, our smart notification system will send them alerts so they know when their parking space is about to expire. Customers will also be able to top up their parking remotely where applicable.

Get more parking revenue in your pocket, faster.

Our automated reporting keeps your entire team up to date on parking ticket reconciliation. By providing real-time insights and next-day payment transfers, your Accounts Department will run more efficiently and save costs issuing and chasing up parking fines.
